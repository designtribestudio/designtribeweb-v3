How UX Research can help to improve your product
Discovery Call
This is where our team will attempt to define your goals, perform problem discovery, and identify potential solution to your problem. We will want to gain as much information about your product, your target audience, pain-points, competitors, etc.
Design Phase
Here, we get to work. We begin designing your product on back of research we conduct ourselves. We make sure that all requirements are being met, while accurately representing your branding and keeping the flow as intuitive as possible.
Build Phase
This phase is optional and up to the client if they’re happy with the designs and want to get it developed from elsewhere, then no problem. With us, in this phase is where the product comes to life. We will proceed to build your product making sure everything is optimised to follow modern web practices, such as speed, security, and reliability.
Launch Phase
Post your approval, we’ll launch the product. To attract and gain new customers, we also perform social media campaigns to build an interest. We’ll also be providing training videos so that you can add content by yourself as well.
